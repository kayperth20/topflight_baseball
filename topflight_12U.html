12U DIVISION

Top Flight is excited to announce a 12U division for our 2015 season!

There are two positions still open for the 12U spring lineup. Fill out the information in the Top Flight Baseball Registration Form , and a coach will contact you to schedule a tryout.

Schedule

Spring season begins Feb. 1st - June 15th.One indoor practice and one outdoor practice per week. Location/day/time TBD. Two tournaments will be scheduled per month, with the exception of Feb & June (one tournament).

FEES

Fees include practices, fields, insurance, coaching,and tournaments.
Players must purchase uniforms.

COACHES

Marcel Johnson & Judah Durham

"My name is Judah Durham, and I am going to be your coach for the 12U team this year. I am an Oakland native who grew up playing and loving sports. I played football and basketball, but baseball was always my true passion. I started playing organized baseball in second grade, and fell in love with it. I didn't miss a single season up until college. I was blessed to meet coach Marcel, and found he and I shared a love and passion for coaching baseball. I have coached t-ball, little league and high school (Panthers, Top Flight, and McClymonds High). Now, I've come to the realization that I'm probably not going to make it to the big leagues : ) So instead, I get the ultimate reward of working with kids, and watching them grow as they build character and learn responsibility through teamwork, while having a ton of fun in the process! Im very excited this season and can't wait to get started. Thank you all, go TFB!!!"
- Judah
