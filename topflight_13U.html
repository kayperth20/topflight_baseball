13U DIVISION

Top Flight is excited to announce a 13U division for our 2015 season!

Our final tryout will be held Dec. 14th, 2014 at Ultimate Sports Academy 3442 Adeline St in Oakland from 2-4pm. Team rosters will be announced the week of Dec. 15th.

Schedule

Spring season begins Feb. 1st - June 15th.One indoor practice and one outdoor practice per week. Location/day/time TBD. Two tournaments will be scheduled per month, with the exception of Feb & June (one tournament).
