ABOUT US

TFB is a Travel baseball organization that concentrates on improving and developing the necessary skills of young baseball players, mentally and physically. Top Flight Baseball instructors mentor clients in the subtle skills of hitting, throwing, pitching, and catching to give them a road map that helps them succeed and excel to the next level of their game.

COACHES

Top Flight Baseball's staff consists of kid-friendly and talented former minor league players, college players, high school coaches, and community mentors. Top Flight Baseball organizes group practices by age, skill level and private lessons.

Marcel Johnson

Marcel Johnson the founder of 'Top Flight Baseball Academy'. A bay area native, Marcel's professional baseball career spans more than 15 years as a former minor league catcher and private instructor. For the last six years he has coached the Top Flight team to 8 division championships, and in 2014 he joined forces with Gary -Lastname-.
