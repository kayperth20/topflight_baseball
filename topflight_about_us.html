ABOUT US

Top Flight Baseball Academy is a year round competitive travel ball program focused on teaching the fundamentals of baseball. Top Flight Baseball currently fields teams in 12-16U divisions that compete in regional tournaments. Top Flight supports development and skills improvement for serious East Bay youth baseball players from varied social and economic backgrounds. 

Top Flight believes that consistent and focused effort becomes a habit that leads to success not only in baseball, but in all areas of life. Improvement by learning from mistakes helps build confidence, great ballplayers, and great young men.

Top Flight Baseball is a proud partner of the Jimmy Rollins Family Foundation. Together we have joined forces and commit to serving and helping the community through Travel Baseball. Our intent is to provide excellent training and foundational skills for life both on and off the field.
