PRIVATE LESSONS

Personalized lessons are the secret to improved skills and increased confidence. Our coach staff combines 20+ years of baseball experience to help strengthen your skills and take your game to the next level.

COACHES

Marcel Johnson

Marcel Johnson is the founder of TF baseball. He has played professional minor league baseball with the Kansas City Royals & the Atlanta Braves as an outfielder for 3 years. Marcel has been coaching youth players for over 5 years, and is also the JV coach at Oakland Tech High School. Marcel offers professional hitting and fielding instruction with the goal of helping kids succeed and take their game to the next level.


Marcel: (818) 738-6289

Josh Wilke

Josh Wilkie's career as a professional baseball player spans seven years. He pitched as a closer/setup man at every level in the Washington Nationals minor league while gaining several accolades along the way. Career Line: 280 games over entire career with a 3.03 ERA, 20 wins, 344 strikeouts to only 107 walks and 50 saves. Josh provides professional level pitching lessons for youth baseball players.


Josh: (770)-653-7545

Gerry Chavarria

Gerry Chavarria has played baseball all over the world. He played in Italy and Mexico as a catcher in the minor leagues. Gerry has enjoyed coaching youth players for a number of years, and his instruction emphasizes the correct foundational technique in his private lessons. Attention catchers - Gerry is only one of a handful of coaches in the bay area who specializes in proper catching technique.


Gerry: (415) 596-9088

Judah Durham

Judah Durham played baseball at Berkeley High and Laney College. Judah has coached T-ball, Little League & High School baseball. Judah enjoys working with kids, and watching them grow as they build character and responsibility through dedication and teamwork. It’s Judah’s philosophy that youth baseball development should include focus, effort, and fun! Lessons include hitting & fielding.


Judah: (510) 699-9678
