COACHES

Marcel Johnson

Marcel Johnson is the founder of TF baseball, and played professional minor league baseball with the Kansas City Royals & the Atlanta Braves as an outfielder for 3 years. Marcel has been coaching youth players for over 5 years, and is also the JV coach at Oakland Tech High School. Marcel offers professional hitting and fielding instruction with the goal of helping kids succeed and take their game to the next level.


Marcel: (818) 738-6289

Gerry Chavarria

Gerry Chavarria has played baseball all over the world. He played in Italy and Mexico as a catcher in the minor leagues. Gerry has enjoyed coaching youth players for a number of years, and his instruction emphasizes the correct foundational technique in his private lessons. Attention catchers - Gerry is only one of a handful of coaches in the bay area who specializes in proper catching technique.


Gerry: (415) 596-9088

Judah Durham

Judah Durham played baseball at Berkeley High and Laney College. Judah has coached T-ball, Little League and High School baseball. Judah enjoys working with kids, and watching them grow as they build character and responsibility through dedication and teamwork. It’s Judah’s philosophy that youth baseball development should include; focus, effort, and definitely fun!


Judah: (510) 699-9678
